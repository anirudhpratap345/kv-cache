## STEP 7: Common Research Paper Structures

Most papers follow this format:

```
1. Abstract (summary)
2. Introduction (why it matters)
3. Related Work (what came before)
4. Method/Approach (what's new)
5. Experiments (how they tested it)
6. Results (what they found)
7. Discussion (interpretation)
8. Conclusion (takeaways)
9. References (sources)
Appendix (extra details)
```
